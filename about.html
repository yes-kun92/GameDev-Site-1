What is Magic: The Gathering?

Magic The Gathering is a Trading Card Game ( TCG ) created in 1993 by Wizards of the Coast which is in the same universe as Dungeons and Dragons. In the game players use decks of cards which represent magical creatures, spells and artifacts.

In the game people use their cards to represent different things like lands, creatures and spells. in order to cast spells and summon creatures players have to "tap" their lands which equal the same amount that is shown on each of their cards in the top right corner.

As players keep playing the game they will learn different strategies to defeat their opponets and will learn about different cards which will help them in doing so.
